## 1. Introduction & Scope

Aura AI ("Aura," "we," "us," or "our") operates an iOS AI-companion chat application that lets you have one-to-one conversations with AI personas. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use and share it, how long we keep it, and the choices and rights you have.

**Aura AI is for adults aged 18 and older only.** The service is not directed to, and may not be used by, anyone under 18. See Section 10 (Children).

**Geographic scope.** Aura AI is launching in the United States first. This policy is written primarily around U.S. law, including the California Consumer Privacy Act as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act ("CCPA/CPRA"). If and when we offer the service to users in the European Economic Area ("EEA") or the United Kingdom ("UK"), the GDPR/UK GDPR provisions described in Section 9 will apply to those users. ## 2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

- **Account and profile information.** Your email address. Authentication is handled by our authentication provider, **Clerk** — you can sign up with email/password, **Sign in with Apple**, or **Google**, and **we do not store your password** (Clerk does). When you use Sign in with Apple or Google, we receive the identifiers and basic profile fields those providers share with us (for example, an email address or a relay email). We also store profile details you choose to set (such as a display name and companion preferences).
- **Conversations and memories.** The messages you send to and receive from your AI personas, and the "memories" (facts about you and your preferences) that the app stores to personalize your companion. You can view and delete your memories in the app.
- **Companion configuration.** The AI personas you create or customize and their settings.
- **Usage information.** How you interact with the app, such as feature usage, session activity, and in-app events, used to operate and improve the service.
- **Device and technical information.** Device tokens (for example, push notification tokens), device/OS identifiers, app version, and similar technical data needed to deliver and secure the service.
- **Payment status.** Subscription and entitlement status from Apple In-App Purchase via RevenueCat. **We do not receive or store your full payment card number or bank details** — Apple processes the transaction. See Section 4.
- **Safety and moderation signals.** Information generated by our automated moderation systems, including classifier outputs and records of flagged content and surrounding context (see Sections 3 and 7).

## 3. How We Use Information

We use the information above to:

- **Provide the service** — operate your account, deliver conversations, and maintain the app.
- **Personalize your companion** — store and apply "memories" so your AI personas can remember relevant facts across conversations.
- **Safety and moderation** — run automated classifiers on inputs and outputs, detect abuse and policy violations, and provide safety/crisis detection and resources (see Section 7).
- **Process payments and subscriptions** — manage entitlements and subscription status received from Apple/RevenueCat.
- **Communicate with you** — send service-related messages and notifications.
- **Improve and secure the service** — diagnose issues, prevent fraud and abuse, and maintain security.
- **Comply with legal obligations** — meet recordkeeping, tax, and other legal requirements.

**We do not use your conversations to train AI models.** See Section 4 and Section 8.

## 6. Account & Data Deletion

You control your data and can delete it from within the app.

**In-app deletion options.**
- **Delete individual messages or conversations.**
- **Delete memories** — view and remove the facts stored to personalize your companion.
- **Delete your entire account** — remove your account and associated personal data, subject to the timeline and exceptions below.

**Deletion timeline.**
1. When you request account deletion, your account enters a **30-day recoverable grace period** (soft delete) so you can recover it if you change your mind.
2. After the grace period expires, we purge your conversations, memories, companions, and other personal information from our **live systems within 30 days**.
3. **Backup caveat:** residual copies in backups are deleted or overwritten on our backup cycle (**≤ 90 days**), during which they remain "beyond use."

**Post-deletion exceptions.** Even after deletion, we may retain limited information where permitted or required, specifically:
- **Legal obligations** — information we must keep to comply with applicable law.
- **Financial records** — subscription/financial records retained for up to 7 years for tax, accounting, and audit purposes.
- **Fraud, abuse, and security** — safety/abuse records and banned-identity hashes retained to detect and prevent fraud, abuse, ban evasion, and security incidents (see Sections 5 and 7).
- **Deletion audit record** — a minimal record (account identifier and timestamp, **no conversation content**) showing that a deletion occurred.

## 7. Trust & Safety

Because Aura AI hosts sensitive conversations, we maintain safety systems to protect users and comply with law.

- **Automated moderation.** Automated classifiers scan both **inputs (your messages)** and **outputs (AI replies)** for content that may violate our policies or indicate harm.
- **Possible human review.** Content flagged by our automated systems **may be reviewed by trained personnel** to assess severity, enforce our policies, and improve safety. Access to flagged content is limited to authorized, trained reviewers.
- **Safety and crisis detection.** We use detection to identify situations involving risk of harm and may surface safety resources (for example, crisis support information).
- **Retention of flagged content and safety signals.** We keep flagged content and its surrounding context **only as long as necessary, and the length depends on severity** — the most serious content (such as content indicating a risk of harm) is kept only for the limited time we need it, and lower-severity content is kept for a shorter time or not at all. Separately, we keep **de-identified safety signals** (for example, the category, severity, and scores produced by our systems, **without your conversation content**) for **longer**, so we can investigate, enforce policies, prevent repeat abuse, report as required by law, and respond to legal obligations. This retention may apply even where other data would otherwise be deleted. (Specific retention periods are defaults pending counsel — [TODO: confirm with counsel].)
- **Banned accounts and re-registration prevention.** If we ban an account for serious violations, we retain a **hashed identifier** derived from that account to **prevent the banned user from re-registering (ban evasion)**, retained for **24 months / until the ban is lifted**. **This hashed identifier may be retained even after you delete your account**, solely to enforce the ban and prevent re-registration.
